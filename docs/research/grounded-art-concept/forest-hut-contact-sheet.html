What the machinery does and doesn’t reproduce

Match the reference; improving the art is a non-goal (ADR-0214 D4 / ADR-0217 D7). These are reported honestly, not reinterpreted — a backend that can’t draw something is a finding, not licence to redraw it.

  • Round doorThe concept’s arched door is a rectangular opening here — the cutter only cuts rectangles; there is no arch primitive.
  • Four-pane windowOne warm-lit pane framed by the opening reveal, not a muntin grid — one pane per opening (the cottage carries the same gap).
  • Sagging rooflineA straight steep gable. The roof primitive is a straight prism; matching the concept’s sag would need a new roof shape — deferred as machinery, not reinterpreted.
  • Surface texturePlank seams, shingle rows, stone courses and the smoke wisp are flat vector fills — the renderer carries no per-face texture or particles.
  • Mushrooms & fernsOmitted — set dressing is separate landscape objects, not part of the building (the cottage & gazebo likewise leave out their foliage).
